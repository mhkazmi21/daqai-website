What We Offer

Cybersecurity Services

Comprehensive security solutions designed for the most demanding, regulated environments.

🔍

Vulnerability Assessment & Pen Testing

Identify and prioritise security weaknesses before attackers do. Our certified testers simulate real-world attacks across your network, applications, and infrastructure — providing a clear risk-ranked remediation roadmap.

🔥

Network Security & Firewall Management

Design, deploy, and manage next-generation firewalls, IDS/IPS systems, and network segmentation to protect your perimeter and control internal traffic flows across your organisation.

📊

SIEM / SOAR / XDR / NDR

Advanced threat detection and automated response powered by the SGBox Advanced Bundle. Correlate millions of events per second, detect anomalies, and trigger automated containment — at machine speed.

🚨

Incident Response & Disaster Recovery

Rapid containment, forensic investigation, and recovery when incidents occur. We develop bespoke IR playbooks and run tabletop exercises so your team knows exactly what to do under pressure.

📋

ISO 27001 / GDPR / HIPAA Compliance

Gap assessments, policy development, and implementation support to achieve and maintain compliance with international security and data protection standards — across all major regulatory frameworks.

👁️

24/7 SOC Monitoring

Around-the-clock Security Operations Centre services — real-time threat hunting, alert triage, and escalation — keeping your organisation protected at every hour, including weekends and public holidays.
