How We Vet Our Specialists

Every professional we place has passed our four-stage vetting process. We only provide talent we would trust in our own operations.

01

Technical Assessment

Role-specific technical tests and practical assessments evaluated by our senior engineers.

02

Experience Verification

CV verification, certification checks, and portfolio review to confirm claimed experience.

03

Reference Checks

Direct reference checks with previous employers covering technical ability and work ethic.

04

Background Screening

Background checks appropriate to the role and security clearance level required.
